BIOGRAPHY

The Breakthrough (1988-2005)

Born in Saint Michael, Barbados, Robyn Rihanna Fenty began singing at a young age. Discovered by record producer Evan Rogers at 15, she moved to the US to pursue music, releasing her debut album Music of the Sun in 2005 – launching a career that would redefine pop and R&B.

The Music Icon (2006-2016)

With hits like 'Umbrella', 'Only Girl (In the World)', and 'Work', Rihanna became one of the best-selling music artists of all time. She won 9 Grammy Awards and broke records with chart-topping albums that blended genres from reggae to dance-pop.

The Billionaire Entrepreneur (2017-Present)

Beyond music, Rihanna built a business empire with Fenty Beauty (2017), which revolutionized inclusive makeup, and Savage X Fenty (2018), a lingerie line celebrating all body types. She became the world’s youngest self-made female billionaire in 2021, and also works as a philanthropist and actress.
